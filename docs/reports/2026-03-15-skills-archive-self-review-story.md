# Development Story: Skills Archive Self-Review

## Core Goal
To conduct a holistic self-review of the 33 cognitive methodologies within the `skills/` directory, ensuring they adhere strictly to the Lean architecture, are free of cognitive and structural waste (Muda), and properly integrate with the core orchestration engines (`context-engine` and `shisa-kanko`).

## The Problem
As the Skill Archive expands, inconsistencies in formatting, missing safety interlocks (Poka-yoke templates), and cognitive overlap (Mura) can degrade agentic precision. A master workflow self-review acts as a systemic "5S" operation to standardize the capability modules.

## Acceptance Criteria
- A comprehensive structural analysis of all 33 skills via `manage_skill_authoring.py`.
- Identification and resolution of any missing frontmatter, oversized modules, or broken internal references.
- Formal documentation of the Archive's current state via the Master Refinement Dossier.
- Validation that the archive maintains a "No Thin Wrappers" policy, distinguishing true cognitive methodologies from mechanized tools.
